# EASE

EASE (East Africa Specialized Engineering) is an Ethiopian construction and engineering company established in 2015, specializing in post-tensioning construction solutions and advanced structural engineering. Based in Addis Ababa, the company has completed over 75 projects across Ethiopia covering more than 550,000 square meters, offering comprehensive services from design consultation and engineering management to EPC solutions, manufacturing, and trading. EASE partners with international suppliers like Rudloph Strong Force to deliver cutting-edge post-tensioning technology for commercial, residential, and infrastructure projects, positioning itself as a pioneer in specialized engineering solutions that optimize structural performance and accelerate construction timelines.
